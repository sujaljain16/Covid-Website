What is Covid-19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. The covid-19 symptoms cause only mild illness and symptoms are common to flu,it might make some people ill and rarely it can turn fatal. Right precautions and rules should be followed by everyone to avoid spreading of coronavirus
